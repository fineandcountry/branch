Property Description

Fine & Country Cardiff is proud to present Foxfield, an extraordinary gated residence set within approximately 24 acres of private grounds in the highly sought-after suburb of Lisvane. Built in 1999 with exceptional craftsmanship and attention to detail, the property combines timeless architectural elegance with modern comforts, creating an impressive family home designed for both luxurious living and entertaining.

The grand entrance hall immediately showcases the home's scale and character, featuring a handcrafted American white oak staircase spanning three floors and enhanced by natural light from above. The main reception areas include a large drawing room that connects to the formal dining room through double doors, creating an ideal setting for hosting drinks receptions, celebrations, and formal dinners.

At the heart of the home is the spacious kitchen and breakfast room, which flows into an informal sitting area designed for everyday family life. The property offers seven generously sized bedrooms, each with its own en-suite bathroom, alongside two dedicated home offices that support modern work-from-home lifestyles.

The upper level of the house provides a large recreational space complete with a bar and seating area, making it an ideal venue for entertaining guests. A separate room on this floor is currently used as a yoga studio, providing additional flexibility for wellness, leisure, or hobbies.

Outside, Foxfield continues to impress with its expansive grounds featuring three picturesque ponds, manicured lawns, mature landscaping, and a private tennis court. A sweeping driveway leads to an undercroft valet-style garage that provides secure storage for multiple vehicles. The estate's extensive acreage also offers potential for equestrian use, outdoor recreation, or simply enjoying the privacy and tranquillity of the surrounding natural landscape.

Location Information

Lisvane is widely regarded as one of the most prestigious residential areas in Cardiff, known for its spacious homes, green surroundings, and strong reputation for high-quality schooling. Properties in this area are often sought after by families and professionals looking for privacy and space while still benefiting from access to Cardiff's business, cultural, and transport hubs. Large estates with extensive land are particularly rare, making properties such as Foxfield highly distinctive within the local market.
